About Me

Eve Hartwell is a UX Designer based in Philadelphia.

She applies her background in human-computer interaction to create user-centered experiences that reduce stress and enhance accessibility. Her areas of focus include design solutions for community building and improving information literacy as a means to empower individuals.

As a detail-oriented professional, Eve's work ethic is characterized by thoroughness and dedication. She prioritizes organization and documentation to facilitate seamless collaboration and drive project success.
